Welcome

Schedule-It is an application buiilt with AngularJS. The main purpose of Schedule-It is to allow for easier shift scheduling between employers and their employers.

Using Schedule-It allows for employees to see available shifts their employers list. From there if they are interest they can pick up an available shift and claim they will work it. You can also back out of a shift that will allow send that shift back to the available shifts. The application is still in the beginning stages and is currently static. Enjoy!
